Welcome to Badshah Yakhni Pulao Restaurant!

At Badshah Yakhni Pulao, we are passionate about delivering a culinary experience that transcends mere dining. Nestled in the heart of Sahiwal, we pride ourselves on offering a delightful fusion of tradition and innovation, bringing you the finest flavors of authentic Yakhni Pulao straight to your table.

Our Story

Our journey began with a simple yet profound love for traditional cuisine. Inspired by the rich culinary heritage of the Indian subcontinent, we embarked on a quest to bring the exquisite flavors of Yakhni Pulao to discerning palates. With dedication and a commitment to quality, Badshah Yakhni Pulao Restaurant was born.

Our Philosophy

At Badshah Yakhni Pulao, we believe in the power of food to evoke memories, to create moments of joy, and to forge connections. Every dish we serve is crafted with precision and passion, using only the freshest ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations. We strive to honor the culinary traditions of the past while embracing the innovations of the present, ensuring a dining experience that is both nostalgic and contemporary.

Our Menu

Our menu is a celebration of flavor, offering a diverse selection of Yakhni Pulao variations to suit every taste. From the classic simplicity of our traditional Yakhni Pulao to the bold and adventurous flavors of our specialty offerings, each dish is a masterpiece in its own right. Accompanied by a curated selection of sides and condiments, every meal at Badshah Yakhni Pulao is a symphony of taste and texture, sure to leave you craving more.

Our Commitment

At Badshah Yakhni Pulao, we are committed to exceeding your expectations at every turn. Whether you're dining in with us or enjoying our food from the comfort of your own home, we strive to provide an unparalleled level of service and hospitality. From the moment you step through our doors, you are welcomed as a member of our extended family, and we will stop at nothing to ensure your satisfaction.
